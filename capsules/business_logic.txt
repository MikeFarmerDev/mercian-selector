Selector AI Core — business_logic.txt (v1.0)
Source: AIv1.0 handover — Business Logic Capsule (filters.py + scoring.py)

Principle — Select sticks that best satisfy journey, priorities, and budget.
Filtering removes out-of-tier and price-exceeding options; scoring ranks the rest.

Hard Filters
- Journey tier map (Genesis → Elite).
- Price ≤ budget + 5%.
- Length ± 0.5″, but only if the right size sticks aren't available.
- Player type match.

Scoring Weights
- Affordability: 0.35  (near-budget u¹·⁵ curve)
- Min Spec Penalty: -0.6 / -0.3 (below carbon thresholds)
- Preferred Bow match: 0.15
- Aerial Bias (triggered ≥ 8): 0.24
- Drag Flick Bias (triggered ≥ 8): 0.24
- Power Priority: 0.08 (if focus=Power)
- Touch Priority: 0.08 (if focus=Control)
- Both: 0.08 (split bias)
Tie-breaks → Score > Price > Weight > Random (for variety).

Template — “These three sticks align with your priorities for {dominant skill} while staying within budget and preferred feel.”
